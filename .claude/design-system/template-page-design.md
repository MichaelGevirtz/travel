## Design Process

### 1. Analyze Similar Pages
- Review travel-site-patterns.md
- What works for this page type?
- What patterns do users expect?

### 2. Information Architecture
- Primary content flow
- Section hierarchy
- Navigation path
- Related content connections

### 3. Consult Design Principles
**From Don't Make Me Think:**
- Is the hierarchy obvious?
- Can users scan and find info quickly?
- Is navigation self-evident?

**From Refactoring UI:**
- Visual hierarchy (size, weight, color)
- White space strategy
- Typography system
- Color usage for emphasis

**From Laws of UX:**
- Hick's Law: Are choices limited?
- Miller's Law: Is content chunked (5-7 items)?
- Serial Position: Important info at start/end?
- Von Restorff: Key CTAs emphasized?

**From Hooked:**
- Trigger: What brought them here?
- Action: Is next step obvious and easy?
- Reward: What value do they get?
- Investment: How do we build commitment?

**From Conversion Optimization:**
- Trust signals present?
- Social proof included?
- Friction points identified and removed?
- Clear conversion path?

### 4. Mobile Considerations
- Touch targets (48x48px minimum)
- Readable text (16px minimum)
- Simplified navigation
- Above-the-fold value
- Fast loading
