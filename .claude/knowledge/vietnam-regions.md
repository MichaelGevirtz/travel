# Vietnam Regions & Travel Reference

Shared reference for regional information, distances, and seasonal conditions.

## Three Regions of Vietnam

### North Vietnam
**Main destinations:** Hanoi, Ha Long Bay, Ninh Binh, Sapa, Ha Giang, Cat Ba

**Character:** Ancient culture, dramatic landscapes, cooler climate

**Base city:** Hanoi (all northern trips start/end here)

**Key distances from Hanoi:**
| Destination | Distance | Travel Time | Transport |
|-------------|----------|-------------|-----------|
| Ha Long Bay | 170km | 3-4 hours | Bus/car |
| Ninh Binh | 90km | 2 hours | Bus/train |
| Sapa | 320km | 5-6 hours | Train/bus |
| Ha Giang | 300km | 6-7 hours | Bus/car |
| Cat Ba | 150km | 3-4 hours | Bus + ferry |

### Central Vietnam
**Main destinations:** Hue, Da Nang, Hoi An, Phong Nha

**Character:** Imperial history, beaches, UNESCO heritage

**Base cities:** Da Nang (airport hub), Hoi An (most popular base)

**Key distances:**
| From | To | Distance | Travel Time | Transport |
|------|-----|----------|-------------|-----------|
| Da Nang | Hoi An | 30km | 45 min | Taxi/Grab |
| Da Nang | Hue | 100km | 2-3 hours | Train/car |
| Hue | Phong Nha | 200km | 3.5 hours | Car |
| Hanoi | Da Nang | 760km | 1.5h flight | Flight |
| HCMC | Da Nang | 960km | 1.5h flight | Flight |

### South Vietnam
**Main destinations:** Ho Chi Minh City, Mekong Delta, Phu Quoc, Con Dao, Mui Ne, Da Lat, Vung Tau

**Character:** Modern energy, tropical climate, beaches, delta life

**Base city:** Ho Chi Minh City (all southern trips start/end here)

**Key distances from HCMC:**
| Destination | Distance | Travel Time | Transport |
|-------------|----------|-------------|-----------|
| Mekong Delta (Can Tho) | 170km | 3.5 hours | Bus/car |
| Vung Tau | 95km | 2 hours / 1.5h hydrofoil | Car/ferry |
| Mui Ne | 200km | 4-5 hours | Bus |
| Da Lat | 300km | 6-7 hours | Bus |
| Phu Quoc | 400km | 1h flight | Flight |
| Con Dao | 230km | 1h flight / 3.5h ferry | Flight/ferry |

## Inter-Regional Travel

### Hanoi ↔ Da Nang (North to Central)
- **Flight:** 1.5 hours, $50-100
- **Train:** 14-17 hours, $30-60 (sleeper)
- **Recommendation:** Flight for time efficiency

### Da Nang ↔ HCMC (Central to South)
- **Flight:** 1.5 hours, $40-80
- **Train:** 16-20 hours, $30-60 (sleeper)
- **Recommendation:** Flight for time efficiency

### Hanoi ↔ HCMC (North to South direct)
- **Flight:** 2 hours, $60-120
- **Train:** 30-34 hours (not recommended)
- **Recommendation:** Flight only

## Seasonal Weather Guide

### Spring (March–April)
**Best overall season for Vietnam**

| Region | Weather | Suitability |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| North | Warm, occasional drizzle | ★★★★★ Excellent |
| Central | Warm, dry | ★★★★★ Excellent |
| South | Hot, pre-monsoon | ★★★★☆ Very Good |

**Notes:**
- All regions accessible
- Balanced temperatures nationwide
- Ideal for full north-to-south trips

### Summer (May–August)
**Hot and humid nationwide**

| Region | Weather | Suitability |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| North | Hot, afternoon rains | ★★★★☆ Good (mountains cooler) |
| Central | Very hot, dry | ★★★☆☆ Moderate |
| South | Monsoon rains (afternoon) | ★★★☆☆ Moderate |

**Notes:**
- Sapa/Ha Giang: Cooler, rice terraces green
- Central: Beach destinations very hot
- South: Rain typically afternoon only, mornings clear
- Avoid long outdoor activities midday

### Autumn (September–November)
**Best for North, risky for Central**

| Region | Weather | Suitability |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| North | Cool, dry, clear skies | ★★★★★ Excellent |
| Central | Typhoon season, flooding risk | ★★☆☆☆ Poor |
| South | End of monsoon, clearing | ★★★★☆ Good |

**Notes:**
- **AVOID Central Vietnam** September-November (typhoons)
- North Vietnam peak season (Sapa, Ha Giang golden rice)
- South improves through the season

### Winter (December–February)
**Best for South, cold in North**

| Region | Weather | Suitability |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| North | Cold (10-15°C), foggy in mountains | ★★★☆☆ Moderate |
| Central | Cool, occasional rain | ★★★★☆ Good |
| South | Dry season, perfect weather | ★★★★★ Excellent |

**Notes:**
- Sapa can have frost/snow (rare)
- Ha Giang foggy, limited views
- Phu Quoc, Con Dao, Mui Ne: Peak season
- HCMC and Mekong: Ideal conditions

## Itinerary Duration Rules

### 1-Week Itineraries (7 days)
- **ONE region only**
- Pick North OR Central OR South
- No internal flights needed
- Comfortable pace, no rushing

### 2-Week Itineraries (14 days)
- **UP TO TWO regions**
- North + Central OR Central + South
- 1 internal flight expected
- Moderate pace

### 3-Week Itineraries (21 days)
- **ALL THREE regions possible**
- North → Central → South (or reverse)
- 2 internal flights expected
- Relaxed pace with depth

---

## Route Planning Principles

1. **Use base cities**: Don't change hotels every night
2. **Travel one direction**: North→South or South→North
3. **Avoid backtracking**: Unless clearly justified
4. **Match season to region**: Don't send travelers to typhoon zones
5. **Respect distances**: Vietnam is 1,650km north to south
6. **Internal flights**: Use for inter-regional travel (2+ hours saved)
